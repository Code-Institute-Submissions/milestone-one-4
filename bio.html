The FireFly Team

CEO - Dan Gunderman

An Air Force Veteran and Pilot, Dan has a true passion for aviation. He founded FireFly in 2017 to take surveys for construction companies, but has since pivoted the company to sell beautiful images and videos to customers all over the country.

CFO - Josh Holani

Josh spent 15 years as a financial analyst with the Air Force, and upon separating he went to business school where he got his MBA. Josh is instrumental in the financial stewardship of FireFly and will lead the company into a prosperous Series A.
